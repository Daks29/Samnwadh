About Samnwadh

Samnwadh is an open-source platform designed to connect, share, and collaborate in real-time. Whether you're a developer, creator, or enthusiast, our platform provides secure messaging, group chats, and end-to-end encryption to enhance your online interactions.

We believe in privacy, transparency, and community-driven growth. Our goal is to empower individuals by providing a seamless and feature-rich communication experience.
